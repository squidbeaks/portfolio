Senior Software Engineering Manager, Internal Tools

Hello! My name is Leah Russell. I currently work as a Senior Software Engineering Manager at a company called Procore. I have been with the company just over 7 years, and I specifically oversee our Core Validation group. This group contains the Performance Engineering, Test Tooling & Frameworks, and Cloud Systems Validation teams. We focus on building internal tools that enable feature developers to focus on building their features, and not the other stuff that sometimes can come along with being a developer.
